# J.O.B. (The Joy of Being Company) — Comprehensive Briefing Document

## The One-Liner

In an AI-displaced economy, J.O.B. is the infrastructure for human transition at scale — a living organism that pays people to do the real work of becoming fully human, and builds the new economy around what emerges.

---

## What J.O.B. Is

J.O.B. is not a company. It's a **Regenerative Community Organism (RCO)** — America's first. It's structured as a dual entity: a nonprofit church (mission/community) + a for-profit Benefit Corp HoldCo (sustainability/scale) with SPV subsidiaries.

**The guiding question:** *What happens when being human is the job?*

**The positioning:** "The Transition Company" (public-facing) / "The New Human Resources" (B2B wedge)

**The KPI:** Aliveness. Most companies optimize for revenue and hope aliveness follows. J.O.B. optimizes for aliveness and revenue follows.

**The Buckminster Fuller principle at the core:** "You never change things by fighting the existing reality. To change something, build a new model that makes the existing model obsolete."

---

## The Thesis

Three legacy institutions — Education, Work, and Religion — each owned a piece of the human (who to become, what you're worth, why you're here). All three are collapsing simultaneously:

- **300M** full-time job equivalents affected by AI globally (Goldman Sachs)
- **15,000** churches closed last year — the most in American history
- **63%** say college isn't worth it — including 54% who have the degree

There is no modern institution equipped to hold what comes next. The gap isn't a product opportunity — it's a civilizational one.

J.O.B. is the infrastructure for that transition. Every experiment is a **Trojan Horse** — legible on the outside, transformative on the inside. The professional self walks in. A more honest version walks out.

## Entity Structure

**Benefit Corp HoldCo** (formerly planned as C Corp — updated to Benefit Corp to align mission protection with for-profit operations)

- **Nonprofit (501c3):** JOB Church — Sunday Night Live gatherings, elder-guided tracks, community, deprogramming courses, doctrine
- **For-profit Benefit Corp HoldCo:** Parent entity holding SPV subsidiaries
- **SPV Subsidiaries (the "many doors"):**
  - New Human Resources (NHR) — B2B offboarding-as-benefit
  - Magic Shows — immersive experiential events
  - Business 3.0 — org transformation consulting/certification
  - JOB Board — human services marketplace
  - JOB Report — AI contribution tracking engine
  - Transition Centers — physical locations (real estate arbitrage)
  - MagicShowLand — immersive IRL locations (separate from Magic Shows events)
  - SpiritTech — technology plays
  - Future experiments incubated within

**Capital structure:**
- Wefunder / Reg CF equity crowdfunding (community owns what they co-create)
- RCO agreement binding nonprofit and for-profit to the guiding question
- SPV structure allows raising per-subsidiary if needed
- Currently targeting seed raise

**The organism mechanic:** Resources flow to wherever aliveness is highest. What stops serving the guiding question gets composted on purpose — no sunk-cost zombies. The Church holds the HoldCo accountable to the question. Current experiments are a snapshot, not a permanent roster.

## The Trojan Horse Model

Every door looks different on the outside. Inside, they all lead to the same thing.

| Experiment | What they think they're buying | What they're actually getting |
|---|---|---|
| **New Human Resources** | Outplacement — a P&L line item | The fastest route from your last title to your actual self — funded by the company that let you go |
| **Magic Shows** | A retreat experience | The fastest route back to yourself that doesn't require ten years of therapy |
| **Business 3.0** | A leadership program | Permission to build something alive — and a map for how to do it without burning out or selling out |
| **Transition Centers** | A co-working space | Real estate arbitrage meets sacred ground — where the passage actually happens in person |
| **JOB Board** | A job marketplace | A human marketplace — where supply creates demand for work that doesn't exist yet |
| **JOB Report** | A performance tracker | An AI-tracked contribution engine that attributes value beyond money — redeemable anywhere in the organism |

## How It Feels

**"Imagine if AA, Meow Wolf, and Indeed had a baby."**

- **AA:** Nobody owns it. Nobody runs it. It just shows up — every day, in every city — because people need it badly enough to make it happen themselves. JOB transition groups will form the same way.
- **Meow Wolf:** You can't talk someone out of their professional identity. You have to surprise it. Magic Shows and Transition Centers are weird on purpose — immersive, playful, slightly disorienting. The professional self walks in. A more honest, childlike version walks out.
- **Indeed:** Indeed built the world's largest labor marketplace by leading with demand. We're doing the opposite: creating the supply. The new human jobs don't exist yet — and you're going to have to see them to want them.
